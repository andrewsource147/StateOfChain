Our Project

A system, where users can post their question and others can answer or upvote/downvote questions. They can also upvote/downvote answers too.
Each votes will be saved in blockchain.
A reputation of user can be recognized by how many votes he/she earn.
Furthermore, He/She can claims prizes offered by system.
